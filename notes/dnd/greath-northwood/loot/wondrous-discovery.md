Wondrous Discovery
==================

The natural-20 result. The find is often watched over by a **guardian**. The tool
randomizes this with equal odds: nothing, a high-severity environmental hazard, or
a boss-tier monster scaled to the party (drawn from `tools/encounter.py`'s data;
see `tools/loot.py`).

## Discoveries

Instead of pure treasure, make it a story **discovery**. These tend to create
more memorable moments than simply handing out a powerful item, so they carry no
item link.

1. **Ancient druid grove blessing** — The old powers of the wood grant a lasting boon to those who tend it.
2. **Friendly dragon contact** — A reclusive dragon takes an interest, offering counsel or a future favour.
3. **Fey crossing** — A shimmering boundary where the Feywild bleeds through; a shortcut, or a doorway.
4. **Lost shrine granting a boon** — A forgotten altar that rewards the reverent with a one-time blessing.
5. **Permanent safe campsite** — A hidden, defensible hollow the party can always return to for safe rest.
6. **Secret shortcut** — A hidden pass or game trail that removes several days of travel.
7. **Map to a forgotten dungeon** — Directions to an unlooted ruin, sealed since before living memory.
8. **A bound elemental owing one service** — A spirit waiting to be freed in exchange for a single task.
9. **An ancient treant** — A slow, watchful giant willing to share a long-kept secret of the wood.
10. **A star-metal deposit** — Raw sky-iron, enough to forge one special item.
11. **A sentient magic weapon** — A blade with its own voice, will, and agenda, seeking a worthy wielder.
12. **A hidden portal to a demiplane refuge** — A keyed doorway to a safe pocket realm of rest.
13. **A celestial messenger** — A winged emissary bearing a quest and a blessing.
14. **A long-dead archmage's cache** — A sealed vault holding one scroll of 3rd level or higher.
