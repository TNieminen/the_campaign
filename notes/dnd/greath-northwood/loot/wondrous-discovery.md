Wondrous Discovery
==================

The natural-20 result. The find is often watched over by a **guardian**. The tool
randomizes this with equal odds: nothing, a high-severity environmental hazard, or
a boss-tier monster scaled to the party (drawn from `tools/encounter.py`'s data;
see `tools/loot.py`).

## Treasures

A rare item or hoard. Roll **d12**.

1. Rare Figurine of Wondrous Power
2. Portable Hole
3. Folding Boat
4. Helm of Telepathy
5. Ring of Spell Storing
6. Cape of the Mountebank
7. Instant Fortress
8. Manual of Gainful Exercise (single use remaining)
9. Ioun Stone
10. Staff of Healing
11. Friendly awakened animal companion with a gift
12. Hidden fey cache containing two rolls on the Magic Treasure table

## Discoveries

Instead of pure treasure, make it a story **discovery**. These tend to create
more memorable moments than simply handing out a powerful item.

1. Ancient druid grove blessing
2. Friendly dragon contact
3. Fey crossing
4. Lost shrine granting a boon
5. Permanent safe campsite
6. Secret shortcut that removes several days of travel
7. Map to a forgotten dungeon
