Mundane Treasure
================

Mundane treasure and valuable objects. Roll **d12**.

Gold values are tuned for a party of 4. Scale coin and object values by roughly
`party_size / 4` for larger or smaller groups.

1. 4d10 gp in an abandoned cache
2. Fine silver goblet (25 gp)
3. Ornate hunting horn (50 gp)
4. Small pouch of gemstones (50 gp)
5. Masterwork weapon
6. Masterwork artisan's tools
7. Bolt of fine silk
8. Ivory figurine
9. Rare spices worth 75 gp
10. Map to a nearby point of interest
11. Valuable monster parts
12. Locked strongbox containing 2d10 x 10 gp
