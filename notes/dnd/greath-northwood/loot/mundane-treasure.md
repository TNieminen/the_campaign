Mundane Treasure
================

Mundane treasure and valuable objects. Roll on the table below (d24, or run
`tools/loot.py`).

Gold values are tuned for a baseline party of four at low level. `tools/loot.py`
(and the web app) **auto-scale** every coin and value amount by party size and
average level, so the numbers below are the un-scaled baseline.

1. **Abandoned coin cache** — 4d10 gp spilled from a rotted pouch under a stone.
2. **Fine silver goblet** — A tarnished but well-worked drinking cup (25 gp).
3. **Ornate hunting horn** — A carved horn that still sounds a clear, ringing note (50 gp).
4. **Pouch of cut gemstones** — A handful of small polished stones (50 gp).
5. [**Masterwork weapon**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=weapon&f=equipment) — A flawlessly balanced blade or haft; no bonus, but worth several times the normal price.
6. [**Masterwork artisan's tools**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=artisan%27s+tools&f=equipment) — A pristine set of a single trade's tools, prized by any craftsman.
7. **Bolt of fine silk** — Yards of shimmering cloth, light and astonishingly valuable.
8. **Ivory figurine** — A small carved animal or idol of warm, yellowed ivory.
9. **Bundle of rare spices** — Aromatic spices sealed in waxed cloth, worth 75 gp to the right buyer.
10. **Map to a nearby point of interest** — A hand-drawn sketch marking a ruin, cache, or lair a short way off.
11. **Valuable monster parts** — Hide, scale, venom, or horn that an alchemist or smith will pay well for.
12. **Locked strongbox** — A small iron coffer holding 2d10 x 10 gp, if you can open it.
13. **Old purse of mixed coin** — A worn leather purse holding 3d6 x 5 gp in assorted coins.
14. **Silver-inlaid bone dice** — A gambler's set of dice, beautifully made (15 gp).
15. [**Healer's kit and antitoxin**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=healer%27s+kit&f=equipment) — A full healer's kit plus a vial of antitoxin (advantage vs. poison).
16. **Two flasks of dwarven spirits** — Strong, peat-dark liquor in stoppered flasks (40 gp the pair).
17. **Fur-lined traveler's cloak** — A heavy, weatherproof cloak well suited to the cold (15 gp).
18. **Carved jade pendant** — A pale green pendant on a leather cord (100 gp).
19. [**A week of trail rations and supplies**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=rations&f=equipment) — Preserved food and sundries enough to keep a traveler a week.
20. [**Signet ring of a minor noble house**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=signet+ring&f=equipment) — A silver seal-ring bearing an unfamiliar crest (25 gp).
21. [**Tattered spellbook**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=spellbook&f=equipment) — A water-damaged book with one still-legible 1st-level spell.
22. [**Climber's kit and silk rope**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=climber%27s+kit&f=equipment) — A full climber's kit coiled with 50 ft of strong silk rope.
23. **Tanned hide of a rare beast** — A supple, unusual pelt sought by trappers and tailors (60 gp).
24. [**Quiver of masterwork arrows**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=arrows&f=equipment) — Twenty perfectly fletched arrows that fly true.
