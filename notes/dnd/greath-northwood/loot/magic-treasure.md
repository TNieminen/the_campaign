Magic Treasure
==============

Magic items, grouped by rarity tier. The tool weights which **tier** is rolled
by party level (and slightly by party size); it then picks a random item from
that tier. To roll by hand, choose a tier and roll on it (Common d16, Uncommon
d16, Rare d8). Tiers map to standard 5e rarities; every item name links to its
D&D Beyond page.

## Common

1. [**Potion of Healing**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=potion+of+healing&f=magic-items) — Drink it to regain 2d4 + 2 hit points.
2. [**Potion of Climbing**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=potion+of+climbing&f=magic-items) — Gain a climbing speed for 1 hour, with advantage not to slip.
3. [**Potion of Water Breathing**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=potion+of+water+breathing&f=magic-items) — Breathe underwater for 1 hour.
4. [**Spell Scroll (1st Level)**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=spell+scroll&f=magic-items) — Cast one inscribed 1st-level spell with no slot or components.
5. [**Driftglobe**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=driftglobe&f=magic-items) — A glass sphere that floats and sheds light or daylight on command.
6. [**Moon-Touched Sword**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=moon-touched+sword&f=magic-items) — A blade that sheds moonlight in a 15-foot radius.
7. [**Cloak of Billowing**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=cloak+of+billowing&f=magic-items) — Billows dramatically on command — pure theatrics.
8. [**Hat of Wizardry**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=hat+of+wizardry&f=magic-items) — Cast a wizard cantrip, and once a day attempt an unprepared spell.
9. [**Wand of Magic Detection**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=wand+of+magic+detection&f=magic-items) — Casts detect magic three times per day.
10. [**Wand of Secrets**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=wand+of+secrets&f=magic-items) — Points toward a hidden door or trap within 30 feet, twice a day.
11. [**Rope of Mending**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=rope+of+mending&f=magic-items) — A cut piece of this rope fuses back into one length.
12. [**Cloak of Many Fashions**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=cloak+of+many+fashions&f=magic-items) — Change its colour, cut, and condition at will.
13. [**Boots of False Tracks**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=boots+of+false+tracks&f=magic-items) — Leave the footprints of another humanoid species.
14. [**Charlatan's Die**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=charlatan%27s+die&f=magic-items) — A die you can magically force to any result.
15. [**Pot of Awakening**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=pot+of+awakening&f=magic-items) — Grows an awakened, loyal shrub over 30 days.
16. [**Instrument of Illusions**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/search?q=instrument+of+illusions&f=magic-items) — Wreathe your music in harmless illusory visuals.
