Magic Treasure
==============

Magic items, grouped by rarity tier. The tool weights which **tier** is rolled
by party level (and slightly by party size); it then picks a random item from
that tier. Tiers map to standard 5e rarities.

## Common

1. Potion of Healing
2. Potion of Climbing
3. Potion of Water Breathing
4. Spell Scroll (1st level)
5. Driftglobe
6. Moon-Touched Sword
7. Cloak of Billowing
8. Hat of Wizardry

## Uncommon

1. Bag of Holding
2. Immovable Rod
3. Goggles of Night
4. Sending Stones
5. Boots of Elvenkind
6. Rope of Climbing
7. Pearl of Power
8. +1 Weapon

## Rare

1. +1 Shield
2. Cloak of Protection
3. Ring of Water Walking
4. Figurine of Wondrous Power (Silver Raven)
