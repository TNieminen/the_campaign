Disorienting Fog
================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** A clammy grey fog rolls in, thick enough to hide an outstretched hand. Sound deadens, direction blurs, and the path underfoot seems to wander.

**Resolution.** Holding a true course requires a **DC 13 Wisdom (Survival)** check (or a Wisdom save against the mist's subtle pull). On a failure, the party becomes turned around and loses a day of travel, with a chance of stumbling into another encounter. Magic that fixes direction, or waiting out the fog, bypasses it at the cost of time.
