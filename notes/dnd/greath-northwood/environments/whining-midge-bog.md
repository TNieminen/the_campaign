Whining Midge Bog
=================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** The air over this stagnant bog hums with a maddening whine. Dense clouds of midges and biting flies rise from the reeds, crawling into eyes, ears, and every gap in clothing.

**Resolution.** A creature that crosses without protection makes a **DC 12 Constitution saving throw**, taking 2 (1d4) poison damage and having disadvantage on its next travel check from the relentless biting; on a success it shrugs off the worst. A **DC 12 Wisdom (Survival or Medicine)** check — using smoke, mud, or pungent herbs — wards off the swarms for the crossing. Fire, wind, or a brisk pace also disperses them.
