Glacial Flash Flood
===================

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** A distant crack echoes as an ice dam fails upstream. Moments later a churning wall of frigid meltwater and debris surges down the ravine, filling it shoulder-deep in seconds.

**Resolution.** Each creature caught in the channel makes a **DC 16 Strength (Athletics)** check (or Constitution save against the cold shock). On a failure: 14 (4d6) bludgeoning damage and swept downstream, then begin to drown unless they surface (a creature can hold its breath, but must reach the bank). On a success: the creature holds footing or grabs an anchor. Scrambling to high ground before the surge arrives avoids it; soaked gear and doused torches are likely either way.
