Fast-Flowing River Crossing
===========================

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** A broad mountain river cuts across the route, its surface deceptively smooth over a powerful current. The far bank is close, but the footing beneath is slick and the water bone-cold.

**Resolution.** Crossing requires a **DC 14 Strength (Athletics)** check; a **Wisdom (Survival)** check can first locate a shallower ford to grant advantage. On a failure, the creature is swept downstream, taking 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage against rocks and arriving soaked (doused torches, ruined rations). A rope line or aid from an ally grants advantage.
