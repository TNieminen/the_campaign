Steep Cliff
===========

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** The route ends at the foot of a sheer rock face. There is no path around within a reasonable distance — the only way on is up.

**Resolution.** Climbing requires a **DC 13 Strength (Athletics)** check; setting pitons and ropes lets later climbers ascend at advantage and turns a failure into lost progress rather than a fall. An unroped climber who fails by 5 or more falls, taking damage by the height fallen. Bad weather or wet rock raises the DC.
