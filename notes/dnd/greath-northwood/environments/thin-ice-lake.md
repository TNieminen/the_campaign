Thin Ice Lake Crossing
======================

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** A broad lake offers a tempting shortcut across its frozen surface. But the ice groans and creaks underfoot, thinning treacherously over deep, black, killing-cold water — especially near the inlets and the centre.

**Resolution.** A **DC 14 Wisdom (Survival)** check picks the route over the thickest ice; failure (or a heavy load, or bad luck) means the ice cracks. A creature on breaking ice makes a **DC 14 Dexterity saving throw** to throw itself clear, or plunges in: 7 (2d6) cold damage, and it must spend its turns making **DC 14 Strength (Athletics)** checks to haul out onto solid ice while the cold saps its strength. Spreading the party's weight, crawling, or roping together grants advantage; a soaked creature risks exhaustion if not warmed quickly.
