Avalanche
=========

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** A shelf of snow and loose scree gives way high on the slope, and a thundering wall of white and stone roars down toward the party. There are seconds to react before it hits.

**Resolution.** Each creature in the path makes a **DC 17 Dexterity saving throw**. On a failure: 28 (8d6) bludgeoning damage, knocked prone, and **buried** (restrained and suffocating). On a success: half damage and not buried. A buried creature, or an ally reaching it, can free it with a **DC 15 Strength (Athletics)** check. Reaching the edge of the slide before it arrives (a successful save plus the right positioning) avoids it entirely.
