Rockslide
=========

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** A narrow pass gives a deceptive footing of loose stone. A misstep — or a deliberate shove from above — sends tonnes of rock tumbling down onto the trail.

**Resolution.** Each creature in the slide's path makes a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking 14 (4d6) bludgeoning damage and being knocked prone on a failure, or half damage on a success. The fallen rock leaves the pass as difficult terrain (or blocks it entirely), forcing a detour or a climb.
