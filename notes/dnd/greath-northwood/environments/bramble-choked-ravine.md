Bramble-Choked Ravine
=====================

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** The trail drops into a narrow ravine that decades of neglect have filled with a dense, woody tangle of thorns — some as long as a finger, hard as iron, and hooked to hold what they catch. The far side beckons, but the only way is through.

**Resolution.** Forcing a path down and across requires a **DC 14 Strength (Athletics)** check to climb and cut through. On a failure, the thorns rake the creature for 5 (2d4) slashing damage and snag its gear, slowing the whole party. Hacking a corridor with blades, burning a path, or a **DC 14 Dexterity (Acrobatics)** pick through the gaps reduces the damage or grants advantage. Heavily armoured creatures have an easier climb but still risk the hooks.
