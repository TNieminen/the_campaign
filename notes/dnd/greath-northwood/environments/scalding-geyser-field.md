Scalding Geyser Field
=====================

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** Steam hisses from cracks in the rock, and pools of mineral-bright water bubble at a rolling boil. The ground is a crust of brittle stone over superheated water, and every so often a geyser blasts skyward without warning, wreathing the field in scalding mist.

**Resolution.** Picking a safe line takes a **DC 16 Wisdom (Survival or Nature)** check to read the steam, mineral stains, and timing of the vents. A creature caught when a geyser erupts — or one that fails the read — must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking 14 (4d6) fire damage on a failure (half on a success) from the scalding water and steam. Thin crust can also give way; a careful, roped crossing or fire resistance greatly reduces the danger.
