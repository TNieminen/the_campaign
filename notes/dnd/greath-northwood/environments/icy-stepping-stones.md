Icy Stepping-Stones
===================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** A shallow stream must be crossed on a line of rounded boulders, each glazed with a treacherous skin of clear ice. The water between them runs fast, shallow, and bone-achingly cold.

**Resolution.** Crossing requires a **DC 13 Dexterity (Acrobatics)** check. On a failure, the creature slips and plunges a leg — or its whole body — into the freezing stream, taking 3 (1d6) cold damage and risking a dropped or soaked item. A rope line, a steadying pole, or a **DC 13 Wisdom (Survival)** read of the safest stones grants advantage. A creature that falls fully in must also be warmed soon or risk exhaustion.
