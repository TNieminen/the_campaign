Weathered Boundary Stones
=========================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** A cluster of lichen-crusted standing stones marks an old boundary of the deep wood. Their faces bear worn glyphs and arrows, half-erased by centuries of frost — a key, if anyone can read it, to which of the diverging trails ahead is safe.

**Resolution.** Deciphering the markers takes a **DC 13 Intelligence (History or Investigation)** check, or a **DC 13 Wisdom (Survival)** read of the old trail-signs. Success reveals which path avoids the worse country ahead (granting advantage on the next travel check or skipping the next hazard). Failure isn't dangerous in itself, but a guessed path may lead the party astray and cost a day. Comprehending the glyphs magically solves it outright.
