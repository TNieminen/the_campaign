Ancient Puzzle Gate
===================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** A weathered archway of carved stone blocks the way, its surface set with shifting glyphs, dials, or a worn inscription. It will not open by force alone — not without a fight.

**Resolution.** Studying the mechanism is an **Intelligence (Investigation)** check; reading the glyphs is **Intelligence (Arcana)** or a relevant language. A **DC 13** success opens the gate. Failure costs time, and a careless attempt may trigger a minor ward (an alarm, a glyph, or a brief restraining effect) before the party reasons it out. Brute force is possible but slow and may damage what lies beyond.
