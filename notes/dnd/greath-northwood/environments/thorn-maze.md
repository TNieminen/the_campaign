Dense Thorn Maze
================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** The trail vanishes into a sprawling thicket of head-high briars. The thorns pluck at cloak and skin, and every gap seems to close into another dead end.

**Resolution.** Navigating cleanly requires a **DC 13 Wisdom (Survival)** check. On a failure, the party loses hours backtracking and each creature takes 3 (1d6) piercing damage from the thorns. Hacking straight through is always possible but slow, noisy, and tiring (and may draw attention).
