Collapsing Ice Cavern
=====================

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** A glacial cave offers shelter or a shortcut beneath the ice — a breathtaking vault of blue-white walls and frozen pillars. But the ceiling weeps and cracks, and somewhere deep in the ice a deep, grinding groan warns that the whole vault is shifting.

**Resolution.** A **DC 16 Wisdom (Survival or Nature)** check judges the cavern's stability and the safest, quickest route. Loud noise, fire, or a failed read triggers a collapse: each creature beneath it makes a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking 17 (5d6) bludgeoning damage and being **buried** (restrained, suffocating) on a failure, or half damage and clear on a success. A buried creature, or an ally reaching it, digs free with a **DC 16 Strength (Athletics)** check. Moving quietly and quickly, or shoring the ice, lowers the chance of a fall.
