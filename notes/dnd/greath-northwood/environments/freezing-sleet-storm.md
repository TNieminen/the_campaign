Freezing Sleet Storm
====================

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** The sky opens with a stinging, half-frozen rain that soaks through clothing in minutes and coats every surface in slick, clear ice. Visibility drops, the footing turns glassy, and the cold bites deeper with every hour.

**Resolution.** Pressing on through the storm forces each creature to make a **DC 14 Constitution saving throw**, taking 3 (1d6) cold damage and having disadvantage on Dexterity checks (icy footing) until they warm up; a success halves the cold. A **DC 14 Wisdom (Survival)** check rigs shelter, keeps a fire lit, or finds a lee that lets the party wait out or safely travel the storm with advantage. Cold resistance or magical warmth negates it; a failed party that doesn't dry off risks exhaustion.
