Whiteout Blizzard
=================

*Environmental challenge — high severity*

**Description.** Without warning the wind rises to a scream and the world vanishes into a roaring wall of white. An arm's length of visibility, cold that knifes through any cloak, and a wind that steals breath and direction alike — this is the storm that kills the unprepared.

**Resolution.** Each creature makes a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** for each hour in the open, taking 10 (3d6) cold damage and one level of exhaustion on a failure, or half damage and no exhaustion on a success. Worse, the party is **blinded and lost**: a **DC 16 Wisdom (Survival)** check is required to hold course or find shelter, and failure means scattering, losing a day, and risking separation. Digging in, roping the party together, or magical warmth and guidance can turn a deadly storm into a merely miserable one.
