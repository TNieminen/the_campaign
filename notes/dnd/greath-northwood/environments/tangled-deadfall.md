Tangled Deadfall
================

*Environmental challenge — low severity*

**Description.** An old windstorm has thrown down a whole stand of pines, leaving a chest-high snarl of crisscrossed trunks, snapped limbs, and slick bark. There is no clean path through — only over, under, and around.

**Resolution.** Pushing straight through requires a **DC 12 Strength (Athletics)** check from each creature; failure means cuts, scrapes, and a slow, exhausting crawl that costs the party hours. A **DC 12 Wisdom (Survival)** check instead finds a longer but easier game trail around the deadfall, trading time for effort. Mounts and heavy loads cannot cross the snarl without being led the long way.
