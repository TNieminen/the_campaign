Quicksand Field
===============

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** A stretch of boggy ground looks no different from the surrounding mire — until a foot plunges through and the earth itself begins to pull downward.

**Resolution.** A **DC 13 Wisdom (Survival)** check spots the hazard before entering. A creature that steps in sinks; at the start of each of its turns it descends, and a submerged creature begins to suffocate. Escaping requires a **DC 13 Strength (Athletics)** check (made with disadvantage once submerged past the waist), or being hauled out by allies with a rope or pole. Thrashing without a plan only sinks it faster.
