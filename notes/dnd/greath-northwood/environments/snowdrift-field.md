Snowdrift Field
===============

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** An open clearing has filled with wind-packed snow, drifted thigh-deep and crusted just hard enough to break through with every step. What looks like a short walk becomes an exhausting wade.

**Resolution.** Crossing the field is difficult terrain that costs hours of hard labour. Each creature makes a **DC 14 Constitution saving throw** at the end of the crossing or gains one level of exhaustion. A **DC 14 Wisdom (Survival)** check to break trail in shifts, fashion snowshoes, or pick the firmest line grants the party advantage on the saves. Flight, a *pass without trace*–style aid, or simply going around (at the cost of more time) avoids it.
