Crumbling Rope Bridge
=====================

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** An old rope-and-plank bridge sags across a deep ravine. Half its slats are missing, the ropes are frayed and grey, and it groans with every gust of wind.

**Resolution.** Crossing calls for a **DC 15 Dexterity (Acrobatics)** check; moving slowly and testing each plank grants advantage but costs time. On a failure, a plank or rope gives way and the creature must succeed on a **DC 15 Dexterity saving throw** to catch the bridge (ending up dangling), or fall 17 (5d6) bludgeoning damage to the ravine floor. Heavy loads or multiple creatures at once may collapse the span outright.
