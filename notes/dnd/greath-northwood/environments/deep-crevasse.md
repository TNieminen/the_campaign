Deep Crevasse
=============

*Environmental challenge — moderate severity*

**Description.** The ground splits into a deep, shadowed crevasse — too wide to leap and too sheer to descend casually. Cold air rises from the dark below.

**Resolution.** The party can search for a natural span or narrow point (**DC 14 Wisdom (Survival)** or Perception), climb down and across (**DC 14 Strength (Athletics)**, fall damage by depth on a failure), or bridge it with rope and gear. A roped climber who fails is caught by the line rather than falling. Skipping the search and free-climbing risks the full drop.
