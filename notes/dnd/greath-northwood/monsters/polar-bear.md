Polar Bear
==========

*Large beast, unaligned* — **CR 2 (450 XP)**

**Appearance.** A huge bear of dense white fur over black skin, perfectly at home on the snowfields and frozen rivers of the northern Northwood. An apex predator with little to fear, it will stalk and kill prey many times the size of a man.

- **Armor Class** 12 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 42 (5d10 + 15)
- **Speed** 40 ft., swim 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 20 (+5) | 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 2 (-4) | 13 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** passive Perception 13

**Keen Smell.** The bear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The bear makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage.
