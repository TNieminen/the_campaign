Young Green Dragon
==================

*Large dragon, lawful evil* — **CR 8 (3,900 XP)**

**Appearance.** A sleek, forest-dwelling dragon clad in scales shading from emerald to deep olive, darkening along the spine. A spiny crest sweeps back from a horned, beak-tipped head, and the reek of chlorine gas hangs about it. It moves with serpentine grace and a sly, calculating intelligence, equally at home gliding between trees or slipping through water.

- **Armor Class** 18 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 136 (16d10 + 48)
- **Speed** 40 ft., fly 80 ft., swim 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 19 (+4) | 12 (+1) | 17 (+3) | 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) | 15 (+2) |

- **Saving Throws** Dex +4, Con +6, Wis +4, Cha +5
- **Skills** Deception +5, Perception +7, Stealth +4
- **Damage Immunities** poison
- **Condition Immunities** poisoned
- **Senses** blindsight 30 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 17
- **Languages** Common, Draconic

**Amphibious.** The dragon can breathe air and water.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) poison damage.

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

**Poison Breath (Recharge 5–6).** The dragon exhales poisonous gas in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 42 (12d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
