Berserker
=========

*Medium humanoid (any race), chaotic neutral* — **CR 2 (450 XP)**

**Appearance.** A wild-eyed warrior of the frozen reaches, clad in stitched hides and warpaint, swinging a great two-handed axe. In battle he gives himself over to a killing rage, ignoring his own wounds to hew down anything within reach.

- **Armor Class** 13 (hide armor)
- **Hit Points** 67 (9d8 + 27)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 17 (+3) | 9 (-1) | 11 (+0) | 9 (-1) |

- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** any one language (usually Common)

**Reckless.** At the start of its turn, the berserker can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn.

### Actions

**Greataxe.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.
