Scout
=====

*Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment* — **CR 1/2 (100 XP)**

**Appearance.** A lean, weathered figure in worn, earth-toned leathers, moving quietly through the wild. Sharp-eyed and unhurried, they carry a longbow over one shoulder and a shortsword at the hip, reading tracks and terrain with practiced ease.

- **Armor Class** 13 (leather armor)
- **Hit Points** 16 (3d8 + 3)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 11 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 11 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 11 (+0) |

- **Skills** Nature +4, Perception +5, Stealth +6, Survival +5
- **Senses** passive Perception 15
- **Languages** any one language (usually Common)

**Keen Hearing and Sight.** The scout has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The scout makes two melee attacks or two ranged attacks.

**Shortsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

**Longbow.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.
