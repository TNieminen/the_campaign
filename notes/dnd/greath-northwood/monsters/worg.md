Worg
====

*Large monstrosity, neutral evil* — **CR 1/2 (100 XP)**

**Appearance.** A monstrous, wolf-like beast the size of a pony, with matted grey-black fur, a slavering jaw of yellowed fangs, and eyes that gleam with cruel intelligence. Worgs hunt the Northwood in evil-tempered packs, and goblins prize them as mounts.

- **Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 26 (4d10 + 4)
- **Speed** 50 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) | 13 (+1) | 7 (-2) | 11 (+0) | 8 (-1) |

- **Skills** Perception +4
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14
- **Languages** Goblin, Worg

**Keen Hearing and Smell.** The worg has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

### Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
