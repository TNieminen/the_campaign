Ogre
====

*Large giant, chaotic evil* — **CR 2 (450 XP)**

**Appearance.** A hulking brute some ten feet tall, with thick, warty hide in dull pink, brown, or greyish tones stretched over slabs of muscle. A sloping forehead, jutting jaw, and small mean eyes give it a dull, brutish look; it dresses in filthy stitched hides and stinks of rot. It favors a crude tree-trunk greatclub.

- **Armor Class** 11 (hide armor)
- **Hit Points** 59 (7d10 + 21)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 19 (+4) | 8 (-1) | 16 (+3) | 5 (-3) | 7 (-2) | 7 (-2) |

- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 8
- **Languages** Common, Giant

### Actions

**Greatclub.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

**Javelin.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.
