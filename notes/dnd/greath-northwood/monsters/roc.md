Roc
===

*Gargantuan monstrosity, unaligned* — **CR 11 (7,200 XP)**

**Appearance.** A colossal eagle-like bird large enough to carry off an elephant, with brown or golden plumage, a hooked beak the size of a rowboat, and talons that could crush a horse. When it passes overhead its wingspan blots out the sun and casts whole hillsides into shadow.

- **Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 248 (16d20 + 80)
- **Speed** 20 ft., fly 120 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 28 (+9) | 10 (+0) | 20 (+5) | 3 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 9 (-1) |

- **Saving Throws** Dex +4, Con +9, Wis +4, Cha +3
- **Skills** Perception +4
- **Senses** passive Perception 14

**Keen Sight.** The roc has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The roc makes two attacks: one with its beak and one with its talons.

**Beak.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 27 (4d8 + 9) piercing damage.

**Talons.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 23 (4d6 + 9) slashing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 19). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the roc can't use its talons on another target.
