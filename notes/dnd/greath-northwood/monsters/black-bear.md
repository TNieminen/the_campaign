Black Bear
==========

*Medium beast, unaligned* — **CR 1/2 (100 XP)**

**Appearance.** A shaggy-coated bear of black or cinnamon-brown fur, smaller and leaner than its brown cousins. It forages the berry-thick slopes of the Northwood and turns dangerous only when startled, cornered, or guarding cubs.

- **Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 19 (3d8 + 6)
- **Speed** 40 ft., climb 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 2 (-4) | 12 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** passive Perception 13

**Keen Smell.** The bear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The bear makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (2d4 + 2) slashing damage.
