Kobold
======

*Small humanoid (koboldkind), lawful evil* — **CR 1/8 (25 XP)**

**Appearance.** A scrawny, reptilian humanoid barely three feet tall, with rust-coloured scales, a stubby snout, and twitching ears. It skulks in the rocky foothills and old mine-mouths of the Northwood, brave only in numbers.

- **Armor Class** 12
- **Hit Points** 5 (2d6 - 2)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 7 (-2) | 15 (+2) | 9 (-1) | 8 (-1) | 7 (-2) | 8 (-1) |

- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 8
- **Languages** Common, Draconic

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

**Pack Tactics.** The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions

**Dagger.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

**Sling.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage.
