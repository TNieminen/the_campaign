Hill Giant
==========

*Huge giant, chaotic evil* — **CR 5 (1,800 XP)**

**Appearance.** A lumbering, pot-bellied giant of dull wits and endless appetite, dressed in stitched hides and reeking of its last few meals. It wields an uprooted tree as a club and flings boulders at anything it can't reach, raiding the Northwood's farms and herds for food.

- **Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 105 (10d12 + 40)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 21 (+5) | 8 (-1) | 19 (+4) | 5 (-3) | 9 (-1) | 6 (-2) |

- **Skills** Perception +2
- **Senses** passive Perception 12
- **Languages** Giant

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The giant makes two greatclub attacks.

**Greatclub.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 18 (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage.

**Rock.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. *Hit:* 21 (3d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
