The Erased Dead-Drop
====================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A weathered roadside shrine to some forgotten road-god conceals, behind a loose stone in its base, a small dry cavity. The shrine's painted inscription has been deliberately ink-dissolved to illegibility — the Ledger's signature touch. Faint scratch-marks around the loose stone betray frequent, careful use long ago.

**Purpose.** The Hollow Ledger ran on information passed hand-to-hand without the hands ever touching. A dead-drop like this let one agent leave a coded message or a purse and another collect it hours later, so neither knew the other's face. The shrine was perfect: a place travellers pause without arousing suspicion.

**Now.** The cavity still holds its last, uncollected drop — a coded packet and the coin meant to pay for it — abandoned when whatever network ran this route went silent.
