Stable of the White Mare
========================

*Kutur ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A long, low stone stable stands roofless among overgrown paddocks, its stalls still divided by worn oak partitions. Above the main doors, a carved white mare rears in faded relief. A stone water-trough, fed by a spring-channel that still trickles, sits green with moss in the yard.

**Purpose.** Kutur's strength was its cavalry, and that strength depended on remount stations like this one. Patrols rode in on tired horses, swapped to fresh mounts kept stabled and fed here, and rode out again — letting a handful of knights cover a vast stretch of the march.

**Now.** The stable's tack-room and feed-store survive beneath fallen rafters, and the disciplined Kutur habit of leaving a waystation fully stocked means useful gear was abandoned here when the kingdom fell.
