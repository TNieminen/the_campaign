Hearthlight Camp
================

*Valvela camp — notable site (inhabited)*

**Description.** A cluster of patched tents and salvaged silver-framed shelters rings a waist-high crystal-engine that glows a steady, comforting blue — the camp's hearth, its heart, and its reason for being. Wards scratched into the surrounding stones keep the wood's worse things at bay. Thin smoke and the smell of cooking mingle with the faint ozone of working magic; a few wary, soot-streaked faces watch the treeline.

**Purpose.** Where most of Valvela fell to ruin, a few of its people endured. Hearthlight is one such rare camp: a band of survivors who salvaged a minor crystal-engine and built their lives around keeping it lit. The engine gives them warmth in the killing cold, light against the dark, and the stubborn dream that Valvela's knowledge is not wholly lost.

**Now.** These are living people, not loot — but Hearthlight trades. They will barter salvaged arcana, charged crystals, and hard-won knowledge of the surrounding ruins to those who arrive in good faith and help keep the engine burning.
