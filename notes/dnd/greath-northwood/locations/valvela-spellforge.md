Forge of Spell-Forged Steel
===========================

*Valvela ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A cathedral-like foundry houses a cold crucible ringed by silver casting-channels and dead rune-bellows. Suspended over the crucible, a frame of inscribing-arms hangs frozen mid-gesture, the tools that wove sigils into molten metal. Half-finished blades and plates lie in the quenching-racks, their surfaces shimmering with patterns that catch light from no obvious source.

**Purpose.** Valvela did not enchant finished items; it built the magic in from the start. This forge fused sigils into steel as it was smelted and cast, producing spell-forged arms and armour whose enchantments were part of their very grain — stronger and stranger than anything bound on afterward.

**Now.** The quenching-racks still hold completed and near-complete spell-forged pieces, and the master smith's vault preserves the rare casting-foci and pattern-sigils that the whole process depended upon.
