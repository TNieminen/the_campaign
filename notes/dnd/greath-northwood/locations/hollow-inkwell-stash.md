The Inkwell Stash
=================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A cramped workshop above a defunct scribes'-guild office is crowded with copying-desks, seal-presses, and shelves of crusted ink-bottles. Many of the documents pinned to the walls have erased themselves — pale parchment where words once stood. A locked cabinet of pigments and reagents, some still potent, fills one corner with a sharp chemical reek.

**Purpose.** Secrets are only as good as the papers that carry — or hide — them. This workshop forged the seals, waybills, and identities the Ledger's agents wore like costumes, and brewed the famous dissolving inks that let a damning document vanish on cue. Its work made the Ledger's lies look exactly as honest as the truth.

**Now.** The seal-press, blank stamped documents, and the cabinet of rare reagents remain — tools and materials prized by forgers, alchemists, and anyone who needs a paper to say what it does not.
