Academy Annex of Refracted Light
================================

*Valvela ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A graceful hall of prismatic crystal panes throws fractured rainbows across its own ruined interior whenever the sun finds it. Lecture-tiers face a central focusing-array — a frozen flower of lenses and mirrors on a silver armature. Illusory motes, leftover and aimless, still drift through the upper galleries like dust that refuses to settle.

**Purpose.** Valvela's great academies spun off specialist annexes, and this one taught the disciplines of light: refraction, illusion, scrying, and the bending of sight. Students worked the focusing-array under their masters, learning to shape light as a craftsman shapes steel.

**Now.** The library of light-discipline treatises, the cabinet of teaching-foci, and the prized lenses of the focusing-array survive in the locked masters' rooms — valuable to any scholar of illusion, and to anyone who can sell what they cannot read.
