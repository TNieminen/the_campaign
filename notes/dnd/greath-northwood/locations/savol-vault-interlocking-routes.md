Vault of Interlocking Routes
============================

*Savol ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A windowless blockhouse of fitted granite sits half-sunk into a hillside, its single iron-bound door framed by carved trade-route lines that interlock into the Savol scale. The interior is a maze of barred bays and shelved alcoves, and a great map-table dominates the central room, its inlaid routes still legible beneath the dust.

**Purpose.** Some goods were too valuable to leave in an open caravanserai — gems, refined metals, bonded shipments — and Savol's route-maps were a trade secret worth killing for. This bonded vault kept both behind iron, with the master charts that let factors plan the safest, most profitable passages.

**Now.** The barred bays still hold a portion of their high-value stock, and the map-table's locked drawers contain route-charts that are worth a fortune to anyone who can still read and use them.
