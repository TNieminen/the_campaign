The Cataphract Citadel
======================

*Kutur ruin — grand site*

**Description.** An immense fortress crowns a basalt mesa, ringed by three concentric curtain walls and approached only by a long ramped causeway built for charging horsemen. Vast roofless stables, parade yards, and an armoury the size of a cathedral sprawl within. Everywhere the white horse and iron crown are carved in clean, severe geometry, and a thousand oath-engravings cover the inner gate.

**Purpose.** This was the anvil of Kutur in the north: the muster-point where the heavy cataphracts — armoured knight and barded warhorse alike — were trained, equipped, and unleashed. From here the kingdom projected its disciplined force across the whole frontier.

**Now.** A fortress this large could not be emptied in retreat. Its deep armouries, treasuries, and the master smith's forge still hold the finest products of Kutur craft, sealed behind collapsed halls and guarded by whatever has since claimed the heights.
