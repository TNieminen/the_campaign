The Crystal Engine Vault
========================

*Valvela ruin — grand site*

**Description.** A vast domed cavern, silver-ribbed and frost-rimed, cradles a crystal engine the size of a keep — a slowly-turning cathedral of blue-violet facets suspended in a silver gimbal. It still moves, impossibly, with a deep sub-audible thrum that shivers the teeth and floods the chamber with cold, flickering light. Catwalks and control-pulpits ring it at every level, draped in centuries of frost.

**Purpose.** Everything Valvela did in a city — the lights, the lattice, the waystations, the forges — drew ultimately from engines like this. The Crystal Engine Vault was a primary power plant: the deep, beating heart whose output ran an entire settlement's worth of magic.

**Now.** The engine never fully stopped, and that lingering power makes the vault both a treasure and a peril. The control-pulpits hold master attunement-cores and regulator crystals of immense value, and the engine itself is the kind of prize empires would war over — guarded now by whatever the residual power has drawn or changed.
