The Dead Conduit
================

*Valvela ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A squat drum of fused stone and silver sits where several buried channels converge, its top split open like a dropped fruit. From the breach, a frozen cascade of blue-violet crystal has flowed and hardened — the solidified residue of leaked power. The conduit gives off a low, dying hum and a chill that has nothing to do with the weather.

**Purpose.** Valvela's engines did not each carry their own power; they drew it through a network of buried mana-lines, split and stepped-down at distribution conduits like this one. It was infrastructure, plain and vital — the arcane equivalent of a watermain junction.

**Now.** The crystallised power-residue can be harvested by those who know how to handle it safely, and the conduit's intact regulator-foci — the valuable crystal valves that governed the flow — still sit in their housings, no longer warded against simple removal.
