Sigil-Scribe's Outpost
======================

*Valvela ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A single-room lodge of pale stone, its walls covered floor-to-ceiling in chalked and carved geometry, much of it half-erased by damp. A drafting table holds the petrified remains of survey scrolls, and a rack of inscribing styluses — silver rods tipped with humming crystal — leans in one corner. Faint blue light still seeps from the most deeply-cut glyphs.

**Purpose.** Valvela's infrastructure ran on inscribed sigils, and someone had to find the ley-lines, plot the network, and carve it into the world. Sigil-scribes worked from outposts like this, surveying a region and laying the geometric groundwork that engines and waystations would later draw upon.

**Now.** The inscribing styluses are functional arcane tools in their own right, and the surveyor's strongbox holds attunement crystals and a master sigil-chart of the surrounding region's hidden magical features.
