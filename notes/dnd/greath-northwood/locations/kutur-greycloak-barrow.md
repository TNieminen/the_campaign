Greycloak Barrow
================

*Kutur ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A long mound of river-stones rises beside an old patrol track, capped with a weathered steel shield bolted to a granite slab. The shield's white horse has all but rusted away, and the crimson paint that once edged it survives only in the carved grooves. Someone, long ago, planted hardy white flowers along the barrow's spine.

**Purpose.** When a Kutur knight died too far from home to be borne back, their company raised a barrow on the spot, interring them with their arms and a token of their unfinished oath. To pass a greycloak barrow without saluting was itself a small dishonour.

**Now.** The grave has never been disturbed — local lore and Kutur reverence both warned against it — so the knight still lies with their honour-gifts and burial arms intact.
