The Balance Exchange
====================

*Savol ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A handsome civic hall of green-veined stone, its facade carved with a vast pair of scales formed from interlocking trade routes. Within, a tiered floor surrounds a central speaking-circle where brokers once cried their bids. Brass fittings, green-tarnished now, frame the contract-niches that line the walls like the cells of a hive.

**Purpose.** The Exchange was where Savol's commerce became formal: prices were fixed by open cry, contracts were brokered and sealed before witnesses, and a panel of factors arbitrated disputes under the impartial sign of the scales. To do honest business in the region, you came here.

**Now.** Sealed contracts are valuable — they prove ownership of goods, debts, and routes — and the Exchange's contract-niches and the strongroom beneath the speaking-circle still hold a tangle of unredeemed bonds, escrowed deposits, and guild treasury.
