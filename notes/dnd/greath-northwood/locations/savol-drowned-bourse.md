The Drowned Bourse
==================

*Savol ruin — grand site*

**Description.** The spires and counting-halls of a sunken district rise from a cold, glass-clear lake, their upper floors breaking the surface like a city of islands. Below the waterline, intact streets of warehouses and exchange-halls stand silent, their gilded signs still swinging in the slow current. A grand colonnaded bourse, its dome just submerged, dominates the drowned plaza.

**Purpose.** Before the waters rose, this was the commercial heart of a thriving Savol trade-city — the great exchange where the wealth of an entire region was bought, sold, and speculated upon. Fortunes were made and lost on its floor in a single morning.

**Now.** The flood came faster than the wealth could be moved. The drowned warehouses and the bourse's deep strongrooms still hold the riches of a whole city, sealed and preserved beneath the cold, clear water — for any who can reach them and survive what has made the depths its home.
