The Masked Gambling Hall
========================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A grand hall of faded velvet and tarnished gilt sprawls beneath a collapsed chandelier, its gaming floor still ringed with private alcoves and curtained booths. Masks — the house required them — hang abandoned on pegs by the door, blank faces watching the dust. Behind the cashier's cage, a hidden stair descends to rooms the patrons never saw.

**Purpose.** Where the wealthy gamble, they bring their guards down with the wine. The Masked Hall was the Ledger's finest harvester: a place where lords, factors, and officers wagered fortunes behind anonymous masks while the house quietly logged their debts, their losses, and the loose-lipped confessions that made the best blackmail in the realm.

**Now.** The cashier's cage still guards the house bank, and the hidden lower rooms hold the Hall's true treasure — the dossiers of compromised notables, their markers and confessions, alongside the pawned valuables of those who gambled more than they had.
