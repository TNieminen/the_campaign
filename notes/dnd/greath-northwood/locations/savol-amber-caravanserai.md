The Amber Caravanserai
======================

*Savol ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A square fortress-inn encloses a vast courtyard behind high amber-stuccoed walls, much of the colour now flaked to grey. Arcaded galleries ring the yard, opening onto lodging cells, stables, and storerooms. A dry central fountain, carved with twined trade routes, stands at the heart, and a watchtower at each corner still surveys the empty road.

**Purpose.** On the long hauls between markets, a caravan needed a strongpoint to shelter in: the Amber Caravanserai gave merchants safe walls for the night, fodder for their beasts, warehousing for their goods, and an informal market where travellers traded among themselves. For a fee, of course.

**Now.** Caravans warehoused goods here between legs of a journey, and the locked storerooms and the proprietor's strongbox still hold cargo and coin left by merchants who never returned to claim them.
