Tomb of the Hundred Lances
==========================

*Kutur ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A subterranean vault entered through a horse-headed stone arch, its long gallery lined with a hundred niches. In each niche rests a knight in full harness, hands folded over a sheathed sword, a broken lance laid across the body. The crimson grout between the stones has faded to brown, and a thin rime of frost glitters on the steel of the fallen.

**Purpose.** When the Hundred Lances died to the last rider holding a high pass, Kutur judged that no greater oath could be kept, and raised this tomb to inter the whole company with honour. It was meant as both grave and shrine: proof that the Standing Oath could be kept even unto annihilation.

**Now.** Each knight was buried in their arms and with their company honours, and the tomb's sanctity kept it sealed and untouched for generations — a hundred sets of well-made gear lying undisturbed in the dark.
