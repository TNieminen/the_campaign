The Pale Picket
===============

*Kutur ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A square timber-and-stone blockhouse sits on a low rise, its whitewashed walls long greyed and streaked. The roof has fallen in, and a toppled beacon-brazier lies in the yard where ravens now roost. A line of leaning palisade stakes — once painted white — marks where the picket fence ran.

**Purpose.** The Pale Picket was the kingdom's eye on the wood's edge: a four-knight post charged with spotting raiders, beasts, and worse, then lighting the chain of beacons that carried the alarm back to the bastions. Discipline, not strength, was the point — the picket was meant to warn, not to win.

**Now.** The garrison's quarters and supply lockers remain partly intact beneath the collapsed roof, their contents protected from the weather by the very ruin that hides them.
