Cardsharp's Hideaway
====================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A snug cellar room, its low ceiling stained by long-dead lamps, still holds warped gaming tables and a scatter of bone dice and faded cards. A bar-nook in the corner hides a cubby behind its kegs, and a peephole-grille looks in from an adjoining listening-closet. The whole place smells of damp wood and old smoke.

**Purpose.** A gambling den is a machine for two things: separating people from their coin, and loosening their tongues. This little hideaway did both for the Ledger, skimming the games while a hidden listener in the closet noted every careless boast and indiscretion the wine drew out.

**Now.** The house bank is still cached in the cubby behind the kegs, and the listening-closet holds a slim, water-stained book of overheard secrets that someone never came back to collect.
