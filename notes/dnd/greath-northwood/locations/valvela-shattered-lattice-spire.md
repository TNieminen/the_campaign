The Shattered Lattice Spire
===========================

*Valvela ruin — notable site*

**Description.** A slender tower of interlocking crystal struts has snapped at its midpoint, the upper half lying across the slope in a glittering ruin of blue-violet shards. The standing stump still hums and pulses with a faint, patient rhythm, as if waiting for a signal that will never come. At its base, a domed control-vault remains sealed and intact.

**Purpose.** Valvela's lattice carried messages across the realm in an instant, spire relaying to spire in a web of resonant crystal. This was a regional anchor: a tower that gathered the signals of a whole district and passed them onward, the throat through which a region spoke to its capital.

**Now.** The control-vault holds the spire's resonance-cores and message-crystals — some still storing the last communications that passed through before the network died — alongside the precious focusing apparatus needed to make a lattice-spire sing.
