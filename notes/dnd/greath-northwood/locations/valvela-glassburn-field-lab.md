The Glassburn Field Lab
=======================

*Valvela ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A low complex of silver-framed cells radiates from a central test-chamber whose walls have been fused to rippled black glass by some intense arcane heat. The ground for twenty paces around is glazed and cracked, glinting blue where stray power still pools in the seams. Instrument racks line the surviving cells, their gauges frozen mid-reading.

**Purpose.** Valvela tested its most volatile ideas far from its cities, in field labs like this — disposable by design, staffed by those willing to risk the work. The central chamber was meant to contain a dangerous process; clearly, one day, it failed to.

**Now.** The outer cells survived the burn, and their instrument racks, sample vaults, and the researchers' locked field-notes remain — including, perhaps, the record of exactly what they were trying to do when the glass began to flow.
