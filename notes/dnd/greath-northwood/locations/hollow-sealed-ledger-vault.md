The Sealed Ledger Vault
=======================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — grand site*

**Description.** Behind a counting-house facade lies a vault complex sunk deep into bedrock, its great door a slab of iron inlaid with a blank-faced mask pierced by a quill. The approach is a gauntlet of false doors, trapped passages, and dead-end strongrooms designed to bleed the greedy and the impatient. The true vault, if reached, is a cathedral of strongboxes, ledger-stacks, and sealed coffers.

**Purpose.** This was the Ledger's heart: not merely a treasury of coin and fenced valuables, but the consolidated archive of leverage over an entire region — the master ledgers naming every debt, secret, and compromised soul the organisation owned. Whoever opened it would hold not just wealth, but power over the powerful.

**Now.** The vault was sealed, not emptied — the Ledger's masters scattered before they could move its contents. The strongboxes still hold their hoard of coin and treasure, and the master ledgers within are arguably worth more than all of it, to anyone ruthless enough to use them.
