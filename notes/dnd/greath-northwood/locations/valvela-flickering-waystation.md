The Flickering Waystation
=========================

*Valvela ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A small hexagonal pavilion of silver-framed crystal stands in a clearing, its transit-circle inlaid into the floor in electric-blue glyphs. Every so often the circle flickers — a stutter of violet light, a smell of ozone, a half-formed shimmer that collapses into nothing. The roof-crystals are cracked, leaking faint sparks into the air.

**Purpose.** Valvela systematised travel as it systematised everything else: a lattice of waystations let citizens hop between hubs in a step. This was a minor relay node, the kind that dotted the network like coaching inns made of pure arcane engineering.

**Now.** The relay's power-foci and attunement crystals — small, valuable, and still faintly charged — remain socketed in the dead machinery, and the lost-and-found locker for cargo that mis-transited still holds whatever arrived but never left.
