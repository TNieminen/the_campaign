Pedlar's Hollow Cache
=====================

*Savol ruin — modest site*

**Description.** A hollow oak, dead but still standing, conceals a stone-lined chamber dug beneath its roots, its entrance closed by a moss-grown trapdoor. A faint Savol scale-mark, no bigger than a thumbnail, is burned into the bark at eye height — invisible unless you know to look. Inside, shelves of warped wood still hold the dust of dry goods.

**Purpose.** Savol's reach extended past the great caravans to the lone pedlars who served villages too small for a market. The trade network seeded hidden caches along their circuits, letting a pedlar resupply, drop coin for safekeeping, and travel light through dangerous country.

**Now.** A pedlar's cache held a little of everything worth selling — and the small, valuable, easily-traded goods left in this one were never collected by the pedlar who knew the mark.
