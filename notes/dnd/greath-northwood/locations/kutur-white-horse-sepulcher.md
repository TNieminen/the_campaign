The White Horse Sepulcher
=========================

*Kutur ruin — grand site*

**Description.** A cathedral of pale stone rises from a frozen plateau, its single great spire shaped as a rearing horse against the northern sky. Within, a nave longer than a tilt-yard leads past the effigies of kings to a sunken crypt of polished crimson-veined marble. Every surface is carved with oaths kept and battles won; not a single arcane sigil mars the place, for the dead here forswore magic in life.

**Purpose.** The Sepulcher was Kutur's holiest ground — the resting place reserved for the kings, grandmasters, and champions whose oath-keeping defined the realm. To be interred here was the highest honour the kingdom could bestow, granted to perhaps one soul in a generation.

**Now.** The honoured dead were laid out with crown-regalia, oath-relics, and the masterwork arms of their station, and the Sepulcher's sanctity made even desperate scavengers hesitate. What lies in the deepest crypt has waited, undisturbed and well-guarded, since the last king was sealed within.
