The Blank-Face Sanctum
======================

*Hollow Ledger ruin — grand site*

**Description.** Reached only through a sequence of layered identities and shifting passwords, the Sanctum is a windowless suite of chambers where a single masked figure once held court over a kingdom's worth of secrets. A great map-wall, threaded with faded cords linking pinned coded markers, dominates the central room. Mirrors and changing-screens line the antechambers — a place built so that no one, not even its agents, ever saw the same face twice.

**Purpose.** Every dead-drop, every gambling hall, every backroom and archive fed upward to a place like this. The Blank-Face Sanctum was a regional spymaster's command-post: the nerve-centre where intelligence was woven into strategy, agents were dispatched, and the unseen hand of the Ledger decided whom to ruin and whom to spare.

**Now.** The Sanctum holds the spymaster's personal accumulation — the master agent-rolls naming every operative in the region, the cipher-keys to the whole network, a private treasury of operating funds, and the single most dangerous collection of leverage the Hollow Ledger ever assembled.
