The Last Sanctum of Mevarre
===========================

*Valvela camp — grand site (inhabited)*

**Description.** Behind a cliff-face illusion and a gauntlet of old wards lies an intact arcane enclave: silver halls lit by softly-pulsing violet light, terraced gardens grown under crystal sun-lamps, and a deep archive that hums with stored thought. A few hundred souls live here — robed scholars, their families, and the adept-wardens who guard the only doors. Outsiders almost never find it, and fewer still are allowed to stay.

**Purpose.** When Valvela broke, its greatest minds did not all scatter or die; a remnant withdrew to the Sanctum of Mevarre, sealed it, and dedicated themselves to one task: preserving the high arcana — the engine-lore, the lattice-craft, the spell-forging secrets — intact, until the world is safe enough to rebuild. It is less a hideout than a vault for a civilisation's soul.

**Now.** The Sanctum is the rarest of finds: living Valvela at the height of its art. Its keepers guard their secrets jealously, but to the rare visitor who earns their trust they can offer master-crafted arcana, true teaching, and access to an archive worth more than any hoard of coin.
