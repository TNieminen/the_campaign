# ⚔️ Kutur

**Identity:** Chivalric humanist kingdom rejecting magic in favor of discipline, honor, and martial virtue.

**Heraldry:**

A **steel shield bearing a painted white horse**, topped with a **simple iron crown**.

**Colors:**

- White (purity, idealism)
- Steel gray (discipline, reality)
- Crimson accents (oath, sacrifice)

**Visual theme:**

Mounted knights, heavy armor, clean geometry, strict symmetry, oath-engraved steel.

# 💰 Savol

**Identity:** Merchant republic and trade network acting as intermediary between powers.

**Heraldry:**

A **pair of balancing scales**, often enclosed in a circle or formed from interlocking trade routes.

**Colors:**

- Gold (wealth, value)
- Deep green (growth, stability)
- Warm brown (travel, trade goods)

**Visual theme:**

Caravans, ledgers, coin-marked seals, modular armor, practical elegance, trade guild insignias.

# 🔮 Valvela

**Identity:** Arcane-progress civilization that systematized magic into science and infrastructure.

**Heraldry:**

A **crystal lattice forming a star**, often glowing or arranged in geometric symmetry.

**Colors:**

- Electric blue (energy, motion)
- Violet (arcane depth, unknown)
- Silver (structure, refinement)

**Visual theme:**

Runic geometry, floating sigils, adaptive armor, crystal engines, spell-forged steel, academic-magical institutions.

# 🕯️ The Hollow Ledger

**Identity:** Information brokers, spies, and record-keepers controlling secrets across all factions.

- Theme: Gambling dens - which are profitable, but also a front. Also money lending, extortion, all that good stuff
- **A front business.** They operate behind a legitimate counting-house, moneylender, or scribes' guild. The "books" are immaculate and public; the *real* dealings are the hollow space between the lines. Every honest ledger they keep is a lie.

**Heraldry:**

A **sealed ledger or blank-faced mask pierced by a quill**, often partially erased or ink-dissolved.

**Colors:**

- Ink black (secrecy, absence)
- Pale parchment (record, truth)
- Blood red seals (blackmail, leverage)

**Visual theme:**

Hidden sigils, coded documents, layered identities, armor with concealed markings, symbols that change depending on light or angle.
